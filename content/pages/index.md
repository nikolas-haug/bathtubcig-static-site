Bathtub cig is the intimate depression pop project of Hilary James. Born in a Minneapolis bedroom, an honest attempt at coping with her long struggle with mental health.

The band features:
- **Hilary James** - lead
- **Dave Power** - drums
- **Skyler Nowinski** - bass
- **Hannah Heble** - synth, flute, harmony

## Discography

**"Old Light"** (2018) - Debut EP featuring synth-pop compared to Frankie Cosmo with raw, honest lyrics.

**"bed"** (2021) - Dream-like and hazy EP musing on quarantine and heartbreaking grief. Solo work with Addie Strei (Adelyn Rose) on clarinet, flute, and bass.

**"Night Blindness"** (2022) - Debut full length described as a clever and dark Gen Z Ode.

**"Red Pine"** (Single) - A joyous sapphic bop about falling in love despite the risk of loss. Press describes it as an "exceptional piece of art" with "gorgeous vocals and guitar work with a bit of a Big Thief quality."

**"Good Mourning, I Love you"** (September 5, 2024) - EP about love during immense grief following the death of Hilary's mom.
